The Bluff works and both Powers and Pryor was released and Abel was exchange to the Soviet who never acknowledge that Abel was a spy. She took his advice and her relationship with her parents get better. Kaira was also growing fond of Jug and at the last session admits to him that she likes him, really likes him and wants to know if they could see each other outside of this office. Four women living in a village in Rajasthan India struggles with traditional cultural norms, society child marriage, dowry and physical abuse.

Rani a young widower, married at the age of fourteen now at the age of thirty two when off to a neighboring village to find a wife for her teenage son. She paid four lakhs as dowry for a fifteen year old bride Janaki, but was dissatisfied when after marriage, she found out that Janaki had cut her hair. Her son Gulab was dissatisfied also because he was expecting a girl with beautiful long hair and refuse to consummate the marriage instead when out to drinking and prostitution. Two days later Gulab return drunk and rape his wife, his mother left the hut and when to her friend Bijli hut and told her that it's her son wedding night.

Rani lonely without any male companionship was constantly receiving anonymous calls, starting out by a wrong number, the calling continue to call and was trying to charm her. She was excited for a while until she became conscious of her status as a widow and it would push him away. Rani continue to focus on making her son accept his marriage and be a good husband and give her grandchildren. He drink and prostitute, which end up with him stealing money from her and cause his mother to blame Janaki.

Champa moved to a neighboring village after marriage, return to her mother house after being abused by her husband and her brother who was also having sexual relation with her. When she told her mother and the elders of the village, she was force to return to her husband by her mother and the elders in the village.

Bijli a dancer entertains the men in the village, her boss wants her to get into prostitution or he will replace her. She struggle with keeping her dignity as an entertainment dancer and not getting into prostitution. She eventually had to accept that the new girl was taking over and she was getting less work and making less money and was force to do what her boss wanted and get into prostitution for him.

Lajjo married to an alcoholic husband who physical abused her on a daily bases. She is look down upon and mocked by the people in the village because she cannot have children. One day while hanging out with Rani and Bijli, they were talking about her being infertile and Bijli suggested that it could will be that, the blame could be on her husband Manoj and not her, because men could also be infertile. This idea came about for her (Lajjo) to be with Bijli lover to see if she would get pregnant. She became pregnant for Bijli lover, and when she told Manoj that she was pregnant he began to physical abused her and call her a whore. Lajjo then realize that all along Manoj knew that he was infertile and cannot have any children.

Krishan a local business employs the women in the village for handicrafts and handy jobs. Both Rani and Lajjo and other women in the village work for Krishan. The women were proud, but some of the men in the village were angry because they believe that Krishan were trying to liberate the women. Gulab and some of the other men went and beat up Krishan because of this, but Krishan continue to work with the women. Rani was disappointed with Gulab for constantly getting in trouble and she had to keep paying money to get him out of trouble. She had to sell her hut. Frustrated, Rani give some of the money to a boy from the next village who was in love with Janaki and to take her away from her son Gulab. She went to visit her friend Lajjo and saw what was happening and attack Manoj. Manoj fell into the fire and burn alive. Lajjo and Rani escape and left the village with Bijli to start a new life away from all the culture, tradition and physical and mental abuses.

Star: Tannishtha Chatterjee, Radhika Aote, Surveen Chawla, Lahar Khan, Riddhi Sen
